title: License Management
---

Low-Ops requires a license key to activate your instance. This key is a unique identifier that unlocks your platform's
features.

## License Types

Choose the license that fits your needs:

- `Starter`: Perfect for small teams getting started
- `Professional`: Ideal for medium-sized teams
- `Enterprise`: Best for large organizations

## Activating Your License

After logging in, you'll see a prompt to enter your license key. If you have one, enter it and click
`Activate with License Key`. If you need a license, click `Go to Licensing service`.

## Getting a License

Follow these steps to get your license:

### <span style={{ fontSize: "1.2rem" }}>**Step 1.** Select License Type</span>

<img src="/img/licensing-service-1.png" alt="License type selection" className="image" />

### <span style={{ fontSize: "1.2rem" }}>**Step 2.** Enter Your Information</span>

<img src="/img/licensing-service-2.png" alt="Enter your details" className="image" />

### <span style={{ fontSize: "1.2rem" }}>**Step 3.** Check Your Email</span>

<img src="/img/licensing-service-3.png" alt="Email confirmation" className="image" />
<img src="/img/activate-email.png" alt="Activation email" className="image" />

Click the `Activate` button in your email.

### <span style={{ fontSize: "1.2rem" }}>**Step 4.** Save Your License Key</span>

<img src="/img/license-activated.png" alt="License activation complete" className="image" />

Copy and securely store your license key. You can paste it into Low-Ops or click `Go back and activate Low-Ops`.
