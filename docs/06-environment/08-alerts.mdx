### Overview

Alerts serve as automated notifications that promptly inform operators about potential issues or deviations from normal
behavior, enabling rapid response and resolution.

You can access the alerts by navigating to `Application > Environment > Alerts`.

### Alert History

Alerts history displays all the alerts that have occurred in the environment.
