### Overview

Teams are used to manage users and permissions. Teams are created for each application.

You can access the `Teams` by navigating to `Administration > Teams`.

<img src="/img/teams.png" alt="Administration Teams" className="image" />

### Team Details

You can add or remove users from the team to manage their permissions. Access the team details by clicking on the team
in the teams table.

<img src="/img/teams-details.png" alt="Administration Teams" className="image" />
