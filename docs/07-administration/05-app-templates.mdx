---
title: Templates
---

Templates are used to create applications. They are used to define the structure of the application. Low-Ops comes with
predefined templates, but you can also create your own templates.

<img src="/img/templates-screen.png" alt="Templates Screen" className="image" />

### Create Template

To create a new template, navigate to the `Administration > Templates` screen.

<img src="/img/upload-template.png" alt="Upload Template" className="image" />

### Delete Template

To delete a template, navigate to the `Administration > Templates` screen and click on the `Delete` button in the
template table menu.
